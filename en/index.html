Who are you?

We are group of privacy, freedom of speech and free software activists which develop new ways to save your anonymity and privacy.

What is you do?

We are working on several projects. You can learn more about our work on projects page or on our Github account.

Why are you doing this?

We belive that freedom of speech can be protected only with a modern technologies and strong cryptography.

How I can help you?

Every help is welcome. You can donate or help us with our projects. And, of course, you can spread information about our work.

How I can control your financial spending?

We believe that transparency is awesome. Thats why we provide all information about our spends and income. You can view it here.
